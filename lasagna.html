Lasagna

  • HOME

    • Lasagna is a baked Italian-American casserole made with layers of flat, wide pasta, a meat or vegetable-based sauce (often ragù), béchamel sauce (a white sauce), and cheese (such as ricotta, mozzarella, or Parmesan). It's a hearty and comforting dish, known for its rich flavors and textures from the layered ingredients. The word "lasagna" can also refer to the pasta sheets themselves.
