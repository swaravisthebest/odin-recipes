Garlic Bread

  • HOME

    • Garlic bread is a type of toasted bread, typically made with a baguette, sliced and spread with a mixture of garlic and butter (or olive oil), and then baked or grilled until golden and crisp. It's often flavored with herbs like parsley or oregano, and sometimes cheese like parmesan is added. Garlic bread is a popular side dish, especially with Italian foodlike pasta or alongside soups.

      Ingrediants

    • bread
    • butter
    • garlic
    • parsley
    • Parmesan cheese

      Steps to Prepare Garlic Bread
    1. Mix the butter, garlic, and parsley together in a small bowl.
    2. Spread butter mixture over the the two bread halves.
    3. Heat in the oven for 10 minutes: Place on a sturdy baking pan (one that can handle high temperatures, not a cookie sheet) and heat in a 350°F (175°C) oven for 10 minutes.
